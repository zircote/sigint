## Strategic Opportunities

### Opportunity 1: Enterprise Compliance Gap
- **Size**: $2.4B addressable
- **Evidence**: 65% of enterprise RFPs now include compliance requirements
- **Our advantage**: Existing SOC2/HIPAA certifications
- **Investment needed**: $500K in compliance features
- **Recommendation**: Prioritize

### Opportunity 2: Vertical Expansion - Healthcare
- **Size**: $1.8B addressable
- **Evidence**: HIPAA concerns limiting current solutions
- **Our advantage**: Healthcare pilot success (3 customers)
- **Investment needed**: $800K specialized features
- **Recommendation**: Prioritize in H1 2025

### Opportunity 3: International Expansion - APAC
- **Size**: $3.2B addressable
- **Evidence**: APAC growing 22% vs. 14% global
- **Our advantage**: None currently
- **Investment needed**: $1.5M localization + sales
- **Recommendation**: Defer to H2 2025

## Threats to Monitor

### Threat 1: AI Commoditization
- **Severity**: High
- **Timeline**: 12-18 months
- **Impact**: Pressure on core features pricing
- **Mitigation**: Invest in differentiated capabilities

### Threat 2: Platform Risk
- **Severity**: Medium
- **Timeline**: Ongoing
- **Impact**: Dependence on infrastructure providers
- **Mitigation**: Multi-cloud strategy

### Threat 3: Talent Competition
- **Severity**: Medium
- **Timeline**: Immediate
- **Impact**: Key hires lost to competitors
- **Mitigation**: Retention programs, competitive packages
