## SOM Justification

### Realistic Share Calculation

**Market Dynamics**:
- Top 5 players hold 45% share
- Long-tail of 100+ competitors
- Typical new entrant captures 0.5-2% in 5 years

**Our Assumptions**:
- Year 1: Focus on 2 verticals (Healthcare, Financial Services)
- Year 2: Expand to 5 verticals
- Year 3: General enterprise availability

| Year | Target Customers | ACV | ARR |
|------|------------------|-----|-----|
| 1 | 50 | $120K | $6M |
| 2 | 200 | $150K | $30M |
| 3 | 800 | $180K | $144M |

**Conservative SOM (Year 3)**: ~$150M
**Stretch SOM (Year 3)**: ~$200M
**Stated SOM**: $180M

### SOM as % of SAM
- Year 3: $180M / $18B = 1.0%
- Achievable with focused execution
