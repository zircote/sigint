**Date**: 2024-Q4

## Executive Summary

## Key Insights

### Positive Signals
1. **Strong revenue growth**: 42% YoY exceeds market average
2. **Enterprise momentum**: 55% growth in highest-value segment
3. **Improving burn multiple**: Efficiency improving quarter-over-quarter
4. **Gross margin stability**: Maintaining 82% despite growth

### Areas of Concern
1. **Rising CAC**: Up 45% YoY, compressing unit economics
2. **LTV:CAC trending down**: 3.2x vs 3.8x year ago
3. **SMB churn**: Logo churn elevated in SMB segment
4. **Cash consumption**: 37-month runway adequate but declining

---

## Recommendations

### Immediate Actions (Q4 2024)

1. **Optimize paid acquisition**
   - Reduce spend on underperforming channels
   - Expected CAC reduction: 15-20%

2. **Accelerate expansion revenue**
   - Launch upsell program for existing customers
   - Target: +5% expansion rate

3. **Address SMB churn**
   - Implement early warning system
   - Add low-touch success resources

### Strategic Initiatives (2025)

1. **Enterprise focus**
   - Shift mix toward higher-LTV segment
   - Invest in enterprise sales capacity

2. **Operational efficiency**
   - Target: 75% S&M as % of new ARR
   - Current: 82%

3. **Product-led growth**
   - Reduce CAC through self-serve
   - Target: 20% of new ARR from PLG
